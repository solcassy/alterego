About me

My alter ego is not just a persona — it's a portal. It exists somewhere between who I am and who I dream of becoming. Bold, intuitive, radiant, and a little unreal. She glows in colors I sometimes hide. She dares where I hesitate. She is made of glitter, fire, and late-night thoughts. Through her, I express without filter, I create without fear. She isn't fake. She's just the version of me that doesn't ask for permission. She helps me take up space — beautifully, unapologetically.
